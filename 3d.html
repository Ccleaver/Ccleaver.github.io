For my final project I decided to mix camera technology with computer technology. The end result is a series of six images that have been digitally altered, broken, or glitched. Each image is of a plate of food, each one coming from a different cultural background. The plates represent the struggles individuals have around food, and the disorders surrounding the relationship with eating. Everyone views food differently, for economical, personal, social, and cultural reasons. For these reasons, I have glitched the images to provide and unclear view of what each meal consists of. In the same fashion, it is not easy to identify and individual with an eating disorder.
